Gujarat Legislative Assembly Results

The visualization provides an overview of the Gujarat Legislative Assembly election results across various election years, from 1962 to 2022. It tracks the number of seats won by different political parties . A slicer is added to the visualization to enable users to filter the data by party or election year, making it easier to focus on specific results or trends for individual parties or particular election years.

Bihar legislative Election

This dashboard analyzes Bihar Assembly Election data. The line chart on the left shows voter turnout trends over the years, while the pie chart on the right highlights seat distribution among major parties. The center displays the elected Chief Minister and party for the selected year. The comparison chart at the bottom tracks party performance over time. Use the "Year" dropdown to filter data and hover over charts for detailed insights.

Maharashtra Legislative Assembly Results
