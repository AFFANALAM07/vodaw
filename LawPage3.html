Explore various Dashboards related to law, legal systems, and judicial processes around the world. We provide insightful visualizations that help you understand trends, case studies, and statistical analyses in the legal field.

State and UTs-wise Number of Persons Benefited

The State and Union Territory-wise Beneficiaries Dashboard presents a comprehensive view of the number of people benefiting across various states and union territories in India. This dashboard is equipped with filters that allow users to select specific regions and see real-time data on the count of beneficiaries. It offers insights into the reach and impact of programs, schemes, and initiatives across India’s diverse regions. Ideal for policymakers, researchers, and citizens, this dashboard serves as a valuable tool for analyzing regional differences in program uptake and impact, contributing to data-driven decision-making and resource allocation.
