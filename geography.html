Geography Dashboards

Explore a diverse array of data visualizations focused on geographic trends, environmental policies, and regional systems across the globe. Our platform provides insightful visual representations that help you understand geographic patterns, climate data, and critical indicators in the field of geography. From global population distributions to region-specific environmental changes, we bring geographic data to life through interactive and visually engaging displays, making complex information accessible and easy to comprehend for a deeper understanding of the world around us.

Lakes of India

The visualization showcases India's major lakes, ranked by surface area, with Vembanad Lake (2,033 km²) being the largest. It allows for a detailed comparison of lake sizes across various regions. Additionally, selecting a specific lake provides an interactive feature, automatically directing you to its exact location via a satellite map.

Rivers in India

The visualization lists the major rivers in India, ranked by length, with the Indus River (3,180 km) being the longest. It provides a comparative overview of river lengths, including notable rivers such as the Brahmaputra (2,900 km), Ganga (2,525 km). This helps in understanding the geographical span and significance of India’s rivers.
