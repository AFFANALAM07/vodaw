Agriculture Dashboards

Explore various datasets related to agriculture, farming practices, and crop production around the world. We provide insightful visualizations that help you understand trends, case studies, and statistical analyses in the agricultural sector.

Nrega Analysis

This NREGA dashboard allows users to explore detailed data for each state and district in India. You can select your desired location to view key metrics like the total number of workers, households worked, budget, and expenditures on wages and materials. The dashboard also shows completed and ongoing works, with the option to switch to full-screen mode for a better view.

Seed Requirement & Availability Crop Wise

The dashboard on seed requirement and availability provides detailed information about the seed demand and supply dynamics across different crops and seasons, namely Rabi and Kharif. It includes data on the quantity of seeds required and available for various crops during each agricultural season.
