GeoPolitics Dashboards

Explore Dashboards related to geopolitics, global power dynamics, and international relations across various regions and nations. Our platform offers compelling visual insights into geopolitical trends, diplomatic policies, and territorial changes, helping you grasp the intricacies of global governance and security strategies.

Aerial Bombing Operations in World War II

The dashboard presents a breakdown of aerial bombing operations during World War II, featuring a world map that uses color coding to depict the intensity of bombing and a table showing the number of bombs dropped across various countries. For example, Germany was hit with 61,744 bombs, followed by Italy with 23,689, and France with 22,226. Additionally, a slicer named Target Country allows users to explore specific countries interactively.This feature provides a more personalized and in-depth understanding of the bombing patterns during the World war II.

2024 Global Net Migration

This Dashboard presents the net migration status of countries in 2024. On the world map, countries experiencing more people leaving are highlighted in red, while those with more people arriving are shown in green. By using the Country Button, you can select a specific country to view its detailed migration data. The Scorecard will display the net migration value. If the background of the scorecard is red, it indicates a negative net migration, meaning more people are leaving the country. If the background is green, it indicates positive net migration, with more people moving into the country.

Note:- Please click on [ ] in the bottom right corner of the dashboard to make it full screen.
