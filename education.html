Education Dashboards

Explore various Dashboards related to education trends, policies, and systems across different regions and levels. Our platform provides insightful visualizations that help you understand student performance, educational access, and key indicators in the world of education. From global literacy rates to sector-specific developments, we bring the data to life through visually engaging and interactive displays, making complex educational data accessible and easy to comprehend.

State-wise Number of Teachers among Social Groups​

This dashboard visualizes the number of teachers across various social groups, offering insights into diversity within the teaching profession. Social categories such as gender, caste, and socio-economic backgrounds are analyzed to highlight inclusivity and representation. The dashboard features three controls: year, state name, and social category, allowing users to filter data accordingly. Two pie charts display the number of teachers by social category and gender distribution, providing a clear view of disparities and trends in the teaching workforce.

Expenditure on Education by Indian Goverments

Expenditure on education by both the education department and other government departments plays a pivotal role in shaping the overall educational landscape of a country. This expenditure is often categorized by sector, reflecting the allocation of resources to specific areas of education, research, and related activities. The education department is typically entrusted with the primary responsibility of formulating policies, managing educational institutions, and overseeing the implementation of academic programs.
