Science Dashboards

Dive into a vast collection of interactive data visualizations that highlight groundbreaking scientific discoveries, research trends, and critical insights across various disciplines. Our platform brings scientific data to life, making complex phenomena in fields such as physics, biology, chemistry, and astronomy easier to understand through visually captivating displays. From the intricacies of molecular structures to the vastness of space exploration, we present key scientific data in a way that is both engaging and accessible, helping you unravel the mysteries of the natural world and explore the frontiers of human knowledge.

Ocean Marine Species And Pollution Level(2000-2020)

This animated dashboard provides a detailed view of oceanic changes from 2000 to 2020, highlighting the relationship between marine species populations and pollution levels. It tracks species counts and pollution (in metric tons) across major ocean regions—Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Southern, and Arctic—showing their evolution from 2005 to 2020. Users can filter the data by ocean region and year for detailed analysis.

Ocean Avg Temprature & Acidity (2005-2020)

The visualization compares the average water temperature and ocean acidity (pH levels) across different ocean regions, including the Indian, Atlantic, Pacific, Southern, and Arctic oceans, from 2005 to 2020. It highlights changes in temperature (measured in °C) and ocean acidity, indicating environmental shifts in marine conditions. A slicer is added to filter the data by ocean region and year, enabling users to explore specific trends in temperature and acidity over time for each region.
