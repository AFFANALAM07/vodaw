Middle East Crude Oil Production in 2023

This dashboard provides an interactive visualization of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in India. Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) is a key source of investment in India, involving non-residents People investing in Indian companies. This visualization showcases foreign investments across various sectors, on this Dashboard featuring tools like a State Button that allows users to filter data by state. a Total FDI Amount in Crore (Rupees) Scorecard displaying the overall FDI Amount received in Rupees, an FDI Time Chart tracking trends over the years, and a Quarter-wise Percentage Chart illustrating the distribution of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) by quarter.

Disbursement of Funds from National Fund to Respective State Funds

This dashboard visualizes the disbursement of funds from the National Fund to state funds, with controls for selecting the year and state. Users can filter the data by year to track financial distributions over time and by state to view specific disbursement details. It provides an interactive platform for analyzing and comparing funding allocations across different periods and regions.

Rate of Interest on EPF balances (1953-2021) in India

The Rate of Interest on Employees Provident Fund (EPF) balances provides a detailed look at the annual interest rates applied to EPF contributions from 1952 to 2022. This data helps to understand how these rates have influenced the growth of employees' savings over the decades. By examining the long-term trends in EPF interest rates, individuals can gain valuable insights into historical savings patterns, economic shifts, and the potential impact on retirement or financial planning in India.

Indian States GDP Per Capita Income

This time-lapse chart tracks the Top 10 States by GDP Per Capita in India from 2010-2011 to the current year. It highlights the states with the highest economic performance, reflecting their growth and development over time.
