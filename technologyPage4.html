Telecom Subscription Wireless & Wireline (2012-2021)

The visualization illustrates telecom subscription trends in India from 2012 to 2021, highlighting both wireless and wireline subscriptions across various regions, It shows significant growth in wireless subscriptions, with states like Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra leading in subscriber numbers, while wireline subscriptions are comparatively lower but still trackable.A slicer has been added to filter the data by telecom circle and year, enabling users to analyze specific regions or time periods in more detail.
