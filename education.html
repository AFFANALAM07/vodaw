Data Visualizations Related to Education

Explore various Data Visualizations related to education trends, policies, and systems across different regions and levels. Our platform provides insightful visualizations that help you understand student performance, educational access, and key indicators in the world of education. From global literacy rates to sector-specific developments, we bring the data to life through visually engaging and interactive displays, making complex educational data accessible and easy to comprehend.

State-wise Number of Teachers among Social Groups​

This dashboard visualizes the number of teachers across various social groups, offering insights into diversity within the teaching profession. Social categories such as gender, caste, and socio-economic backgrounds are analyzed to highlight inclusivity and representation. The dashboard features three controls: year, state name, and social category, allowing users to filter data accordingly. Two pie charts display the number of teachers by social category and gender distribution, providing a clear, interactive view of disparities and trends in the teaching workforce.

Expenditure on Education by Indian Goverments

Expenditure on education by both the education department and other government departments plays a pivotal role in shaping the overall educational landscape of a country. This expenditure is often categorized by sector, reflecting the allocation of resources to specific areas of education, research, and related activities. The education department is typically entrusted with the primary responsibility of formulating policies, managing educational institutions, and overseeing the implementation of academic programs.

National & State Universities in India

This dashboard on "National & State Universities in India" offers an intuitive way to explore and analyze data related to different types of universities across the country. It categorizes universities into groups like Central Universities, State Public Universities, and Deemed Universities, allowing users to easily filter and view specific categories. The dashboard provides a state-wise distribution of universities, where users can interact with the visual elements to see the number of institutions in each state. Users can compare university types across different states using charts and other visual aids. By clicking on various charts and filters, users can dynamically adjust the data presented to focus on their specific areas of interest. This makes it simple for users to understand the higher education landscape in India.

Enrolment through Distance Mode at Different Levels

This dashboard presents state-wise enrollment data for undergraduate (UG), diploma, and postgraduate diploma (PG-Diploma) levels, separated by gender for each state or union territory in 2021-22. The top section shows enrollment figures at the UG level, with the distribution of male and female students displayed as stacked bar charts for each state. Similarly, the middle section represents diploma-level enrollments, and the bottom section focuses on PG-Diploma levels. The blue bars indicate male enrollments, while the pink bars represent female enrollments, allowing for a clear comparison of gender distribution across different educational levels and states. Users can easily identify the total enrollment figures and gender proportion in each category by observing the respective bars.
