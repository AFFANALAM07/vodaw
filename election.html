Data Visualizations Related to Election

Explore various datasets related to Election result, Electrol History, in the India. We provide insightful visualizations that help you understand trends, case studies, and statistical analyses in this field.

Gujarat Legislative Assembly Results

The visualization provides an overview of the Gujarat Legislative Assembly election results across various election years, from 1962 to 2022. It tracks the number of seats won by different political parties . A slicer is added to the visualization to enable users to filter the data by party or election year, making it easier to focus on specific results or trends for individual parties or particular election years.

Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly Results

The visualization provides a detailed breakdown of the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly election results over years, It tracks the performance of various political parties like the INC, BJP, SP, and BSP, showing the number of seats won by each party during different election cycles, offering a historical overview of political trends and shifts in the state. A slider has been added to filter the results by election years.
