Instagram User Trends (2023-2024)

Intrduction :-
Instagram continues to be one of the most widely used social media platforms globally, with its user base expanding significantly across different regions. This report analyzes Instagram user trends in the top 20 countries for 2023 and 2024, along with gender-based usage insights. The data highlights country-wise growth, gender disparities, and regional user engagement shifts.
Dashboard :-
The visualization presents-:
Top 20 Countries by Instagram Users: A bar chart comparing user numbers in 2023 vs. 2024.
Male vs. Female User Ratios: Separate visualizations ranking countries by gender dominance.
Growth Trends: Percentage increase in user numbers, showing expansion in emerging markets.
Slicer Functionality:The slicer enables users to filter data by country, year, and gender distribution, allowing for customized analysis of specific trends and comparisons.

Instagram Users by Country :-
1) India:
• 2023: 229.6M users
• 2024: 392.5M users
• Growth: +70.9%
• Observation: India remains the largest Instagram market, showing massive adoption and engagement growth.

2) United States:
• 2023: 143.4M users
• 2024: 172.6M users
• Growth: +20.3%
• Observation: Consistent growth, maintaining its position as the second-largest market.

3) Brazil:
• 2023: 113.5M users
• 2024: 141.4M users
• Growth: +24.6%
• Observation: Strong social media engagement across Latin America.

4) Other Notable Countries:
• Indonesia: Grew from 89.2M to 90.2M (+1.1%)
• Turkey: Increased from 48.7M to 58.3M (+19.7%)
• Mexico, UK, Germany, Italy, and France: All showed steady growth.

Gender-Based User Distribution :-
Instagram’s gender distribution varies significantly across different regions. The dataset categorizes the top 20 male-dominated and female-dominated countries:
Male-Dominated Countries (Percentage of Male Users in 2024):
• Chad: 80.8%
• Afghanistan: 78.9%
• Tajikistan: 76.1%
• Bangladesh: 68.7%
• India: 66.9%
• Pakistan: 64.9%

Female-Dominated Countries (Percentage of Female Users in 2024):
• Belarus: 66.1%
• Ukraine: 63.4%
• Latvia: 61.8%
• Thailand: 60.6%
• Poland: 60.2%
• Denmark: 58.5%

Conclusion :-
The report highlights Instagram’s significant growth in India and other emerging markets while showcasing gender disparities in different regions. The visual representation helps in identifying user demographics and market trends, offering valuable insights for digital marketers and social media analysts.
