Seed Requirement & Availability Crop Wise

The dataset on seed requirement and availability provides detailed information about the seed demand and supply dynamics across different crops and seasons, namely Rabi and Kharif. It includes data on the quantity of seeds required and available for various crops during each agricultural season.

Distribution of agricultural machines

The Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) aims to improve farm mechanization access for small and marginal farmers by establishing Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs) and Farm Machinery Banks. It provides subsidized equipment, machine hiring options, and promotes awareness through demonstrations and skill development. SMAM also ensures the quality of machinery through testing at Farm Machinery Training and Testing Institutes (FMTTI), State Agricultural Universities (SAU), and Indian Agricultural Research Institute (ICAR) centers. The initiative helps address the high costs of machine ownership and small landholdings by promoting shared-use equipment.

Estimated Area, Production and Yield of Crops

Estimated Area, Production and Yield of Crops like cereals, cotton, tur, pulses, Bajra, etc. in kilogram per hectare which may vary from year to year depending upon weather conditions including temporal and spatial spread of rainfall, farmers perception of demand and supply of various agricultural products, emerging international scenario and policies of Union and State Governments.
