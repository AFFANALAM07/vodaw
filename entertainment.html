Entertainment Dashboards

Explore a dynamic collection of interactive Dashboards that spotlight major milestones in entertainment, including film, music, gaming, and more. Our platform transforms entertainment data into engaging visual displays, making trends, box office performances, and audience preferences easier to understand. From the evolution of cinematic storytelling to the rise of streaming services and global gaming phenomena, we present entertainment data in a captivating and accessible way, allowing you to dive deeper into the industry’s biggest hits and cultural shifts shaping today’s entertainment landscape.

Top Movies by IMDB Rating

The visualization presents the top 100 movies based on IMDb ratings, including classics like The Shawshank Redemption (9.3 rating), The Godfather (9.2), and The Dark Knight (9.0). Each movie is accompanied by details such as genre, description, year of release, and IMDb rating. A slicer allows users to filter movies by title or year, enabling focused exploration of specific films or time periods.

Tracks Streams on Spotify

The visualization is a bar chart showcasing Spotify's "Top 50 Tracks" ranked by stream count, with values ranging from 0 to 4 billion streams. The chart is accompanied by two interactive buttons labeled "Track Name" and "Released Year." These buttons allow users to filter the displayed data based on their preferred track or release year. users can explore specific songs providing a dynamic and customizable view of the most-streamed music.
