Sport Dashboards

Explore a variety of datasets related to sports, athletes, and competitions from around the world. We provide insightful visualizations that highlight trends, performance analytics, and statistical comparisons across different sports. Dive into detailed case studies, team dynamics, and individual player achievements through our dynamic data visualizations in the sports field.

Summer Olympic Dashboard

The Summer Olympic Dashboard provides an interactive overview of the medal counts for every country. Using the slicer, you can easily filter and view the number of gold, silver, and bronze medals won by each nation. Additionally, a bar chart displays the total number of medals won.

The Most famous Sports in the World

A survey conducted by a sports company to determine the most popular sports, as shown in the chart, with the Y-axis representing the number of fans in billions and the X-axis representing all the famous sports, places football at the top, with an estimated 4 billion fans.

Distribution of FIFA World Cup Wins by Country

This 3D pie chart visually represents the number of FIFA World Cup titles won by various countries. Each segment of the pie corresponds to a nation that has lifted the prestigious trophy. The numbers inside each slice indicate the total wins

Countries in Summer Olympics Medal Wins (1896-2024)

This animated chart showcases the dynamic rise and fall of the top 10 countries in total medal counts at the Summer Olympics, spanning multiple decades.When you hover over the chart, the animation starts playing, When you move your mouse away, the animation stops.
