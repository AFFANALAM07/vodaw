Economics Dashboards

Explore various Data Visualizations related to economic trends, policies, and global financial systems across different regions and industries. Our platform provides insightful visualizations that help you understand market dynamics, key economic indicators, and statistical analyses in the world of economics. From international trade patterns to sector-specific economic developments, we bring the data to life through visually engaging and interactive displays, making complex financial data accessible and comprehensible.

State & UT wise List of Disbursement of Funds from National Fund to Respective State Funds

This dashboard visualizes the disbursement of funds from the National Fund to state funds, with controls for selecting the year and state. Users can filter the data by year to track financial distributions over time and by state to view specific disbursement details. It provides an interactive platform for analyzing and comparing funding allocations across different periods and regions

Rate of Interest on EPF balances (1953-2021) in India

The Rate of Interest on Employees Provident Fund (EPF) balances provides a detailed look at the annual interest rates applied to EPF contributions from 1952 to 2022. This data helps to understand how these rates have influenced the growth of employees' savings over the decades. By examining the long-term trends in EPF interest rates, individuals can gain valuable insights into historical savings patterns, economic shifts, and the potential impact on retirement or financial planning in India.
