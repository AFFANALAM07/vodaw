Deaths in conflicts Across World

This Dashboard shows the number of Deaths in Conflicts from 1989 to 2022 in different countries. It highlights Countries with the most deaths, like Rwanda with 794,913, Syria with 402,416 etc . it makes it easy to see the human cost of these conflicts and compare the number of fatalities across regions. You’ll also find a world map that helps you see where the deaths happened and a slicer tool that lets you filter for specific countries you want to explore.

Deaths in World War I Across World

This dashboard displays the deaths during World War I, categorized by nationality. Countries such as Russia (3,394,369 deaths), Turkey (3,271,844), and Germany (2,800,720) had the highest fatalities. You Can Also Compares the total death tolls from various nations By Selecting Multiple Countries, You’ll also find a world map that helps you see where the deaths happened. Also a Button Named Country that lets you filter for specific countries you want to See.

Note:- Please click on [ ] in the bottom right corner of the dashboard to make it full screen.
