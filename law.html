State wise victim compensation
(2016-2021)

This dashboard provides an interactive visualization of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in India. Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) is a key source of investment in India, involving non-residents People investing in Indian companies. This visualization showcases foreign investments across various sectors, on this Dashboard featuring tools like a State Button that allows users to filter data by state. a Total FDI Amount in Crore (Rupees) Scorecard displaying the overall FDI Amount received in Rupees, an FDI Time Chart tracking trends over the years, and a Quarter-wise Percentage Chart illustrating the distribution of Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) by quarter.
