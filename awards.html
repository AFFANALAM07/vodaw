Award Dashboards

Explore a diverse range of Dashboards related to awards and recognitions across various fields. Our platform offers insightful visualizations that illuminate trends, statistics, and patterns in award distributions. By transforming complex data into engaging graphics, we help you understand the impact of awards on industries, demographics, and achievements, making the information accessible and meaningful.

Nobel Winners in Field of Chemistry

The Nobel Prize Winners in Chemistry Dashboard is an interactive platform that showcases the achievements of Chemistry laureates. Users can filter the dataset by Year, Country, and Gender, gaining insights into award distributions, trends over time, and gender representation among winners. Intuitive slicers allow for easy customization such as the number of female laureates from different countries.

Nobel Winners in Field of Economics

The Nobel Prize Winners in Economics Dashboard is an interactive platform that highlights the achievements of Nobel laureates in Economics. Users can filter the dataset by Year, Country, and Gender, providing insights into award distributions, trends over time, and gender representation among winners. Intuitive slicers facilitate easy customization, allowing users to view specific winners such as the number of female laureates from various countries.
