List of State having Nuclear Weapons

This dashboard offers an expansive and detailed visualization of the global distribution of nuclear weapons, clearly illustrating the number possessed by each country on a world map. Countries that do not possess any nuclear weapons are prominently displayed in white, providing a stark contrast and emphasizing the difference in nuclear capabilities across nations.

Indian Population Growth

The time-lapse chart of India's population growth from 1955 to 2024 visually captures the expansion of the country's population over more than six decades. Starting in 1960, the chart shows how India's population steadily increased year after year. In the early decades, the growth rate was rapid due to high birth rates and improved healthcare.
