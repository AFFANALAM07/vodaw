Agriculture Dashboards

Explore various datasets related to agriculture, farming practices, and crop production around the world. We provide insightful visualizations that help you understand trends, case studies, and statistical analyses in the agricultural sector.

Distribution of agricultural machines

The Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM) promotes shared-use agricultural equipment by distributing machines across Indian states through Custom Hiring Centers (CHCs) and Farm Machinery Banks .This interactive dashboard visualizes state-wise and year-wise trends in machine distribution from 2014 to 2021, featuring filters for selecting specific years and states. A line chart highlights distribution trends across states, while pie charts provide year-wise and state-wise percentage breakdowns of machine allocations.

Estimated Area, Production and Yield of Crops

Estimated Area, Production and Yield of Crops like cereals, cotton, tur, pulses, Bajra, etc. in kilogram per hectare which may vary from year to year depending upon weather conditions including temporal and spatial spread of rainfall, farmers perception of demand and supply of various agricultural products, emerging international scenario and policies of Union and State Governments. This interactive dashboard visualizes state-wise and year-wise trends and also crop Name and their category.
