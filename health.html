Data Visualizations Related to Health

Explore various Data Visualizations related to health trends, policies, and global healthcare systems across different regions and sectors. Our platform offers insightful visualizations that help you understand public health dynamics, key health indicators, and statistical analyses in the medical field. From disease prevalence patterns to healthcare accessibility and advancements, we bring the data to life through visually engaging and interactive displays, making complex health data accessible and easy to comprehend.

Cholera Cases & corresponding deaths globally in 2024

This visualization showcases the global impact of cholera in 2024, highlighting total cases and deaths across various countries. providing a clear picture of how cholera has affected different regions. The data illustrates the spread of the disease, with figures for both total cases and deaths due to cholera. A slicer Name Country allows you to filter and explore specific countries, offering a more focused view of the cholera outbreak’s toll. This interactive feature helps you delve deeper into each region’s battle with the disease, bringing the numbers to life.

COVID-19 Cases and Deaths around the World

The visualization displays COVID-19 cases and deaths around the world, with countries such as China, France, Brazil, and Japan being highlighted in the charts. The charts represent the total number of cases and total deaths globally and for individual countries, providing a comprehensive view of the pandemic's reach and impact across regions.A slicer Named Country has been added to the dashboard, allowing users to filter the data by Country. This feature enables a more focused analysis of the data, making it easier to explore COVID-19 statistics for specific countries.

Dengue Cases & Deaths in india from 2020 to 2024

The number of dengue cases is shown for various states like Punjab, Kerala, Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, etc across the years 2020 to 2024(Up till 30-06-2024). The visual represents the number of cases in each state, with trends indicating the rise or fall in cases over the years. Similar to the cases, this section highlights the corresponding dengue-related deaths across the same states for the years 2020 to 2024(Up till 30-06-2024).A slicer Named State is added to the visualization to enable users to filter the data by specific states

Life expectancy at birth Across World (1961-2021)

The visualization depicts the life expectancy at birth for various countries from 1961 to 2021, showcasing trends in longevity over time. It compares life expectancy across different regions, such as the Netherlands, Canada, Italy, and many others, with clear upward trajectories for most countries. For instance, developed nations show higher life expectancies, while certain lower-income countries have seen slower growth in this metric.
