Delhi legislative Election

OVERVIEW -:
This interactive dashboard provides insights into Delhi Assembly Elections, allowing users to explore results by selecting different years. It includes:
● Election Year Selection
● Voter Turnout Percentage
● Winning Party & Elected CM
● Seat Distribution (Pie Chart)
● Seats Won & Vote Share Trends (Line Charts)

How to Use the Dashboard?
Select an Election Year from the slicer to instantly update all visuals. The dashboard will dynamically display voter turnout, the winning party, and the elected Chief Minister for the selected year. The pie chart will reflect the seat distribution, while the line charts will update to show historical trends.
