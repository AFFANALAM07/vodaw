Law Dashboards

Explore various Dashboards related to law, legal systems, and judicial processes around the world. We provide insightful visualizations that help you understand trends, case studies, and statistical analyses in the legal field.

State wise victim compensation (2016-2021)

This dashboard visualizes victim compensation and application decisions across Indian states from 2016 to 2021. At the top, dropdown filters allow users to select a specific State Legal Services Authority and financial year to customize the data view. The first chart displays state-wise compensation amounts, where blue bars indicate the funds received by each state. The second chart shows the number of applications decided, with different colors representing various years. Users can hover over the charts to see detailed values. Built in Looker Studio, this interactive dashboard enables easy exploration of victim compensation trends.

State wise functional Gram Nyayalayas (Rural Court)

This dashboard displays the number of functional Gram Nyayalayas (Rural Courts) in India for 2021-2022. The dropdown filter at the top allows users to select a specific state for a focused view. The bar chart represents the distribution, with Madhya Pradesh leading at 89 courts, followed by Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh, and Kerala. States like Goa, Telangana, and Ladakh have no functional rural . Users can hover over the bars to see exact counts.

Note:- Please click on [ ] in the bottom right corner of the dashboard to make it full screen.
