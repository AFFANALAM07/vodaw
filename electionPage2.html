Jharkhand Legislative Assembly Results

The visualization provides an overview of the Jharkhand Legislative Assembly election results across various election years It tracks the number of seats won by different political parties . A slicer is added to the visualization to enable users to filter the data by party or election year, making it easier to focus on specific results or trends for individual parties or particular election years.

Maharashtra Legislative Assembly Results

The visualization provides a detailed breakdown of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly election results over years, It tracks the performance of various political parties showing the number of seats won by each party during different election cycles, offering a historical overview of political trends and shifts in the state. A slider has been added to filter the results by election years.
