Roman Rulers & respective Reign

Ottomon Sultans & Respective Period

The visualization presents a timeline of the reigns of various Ottoman sultans, showing the start and end of their rule, including figures like Osman I, Murad I, Mehmed I, and others. Each sultan's reign is depicted in chronological order, illustrating the longevity of their rule and the transitions between them. For example, Osman I ruled from 1299 to 1324, and Mehmed I reigned from 1413 to 1421 after the Ottoman Civil War.And If You Want See a specific Sultan Just Use Sultan Button To display About the Specific Sulta
