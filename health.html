COVID-19 Dashboard

The visualization displays COVID-19 cases and deaths around the world, with The charts represent the total number of cases and total deaths globally and for individual countries, providing a comprehensive view of the pandemic's reach and impact across regions.A slicer Named Country has been added to the dashboard, allowing users to filter the data by Country.
