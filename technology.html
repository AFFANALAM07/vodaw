Data Visualizations Related to Technology

Explore a wide range of Data Visualizations related to technology innovations, advancements, and global tech trends across various industries and regions. Our platform offers compelling visual insights into emerging technologies, AI development, cybersecurity, and tech-driven economies, helping you understand the impact of digital transformation, technological disruption, and future trends shaping the modern world.

India's Export of Electronic Commodity (2021-2022)

The visualization shows India's export and re-export of electronic commodities for the fiscal year 2021-2022. It highlights the export values of various electronic goods such as SIM cards, aerials, set-top boxes, and mobile phones, with a total export value of ₹4,445.65 crore. Commodities like SIM cards and aerial products dominate the export chart, while other items contribute smaller amounts.A slicer has been added to filter the data by specific commodities, allowing users to explore the export figures of individual electronic goods in greater detail.

India's Import Electronic Commodity (2021-2022)

The visualization shows India's import and re-import of electronic commodities for the fiscal year 2021-2022. It presents the total import value of various electronic products, with items like mobile phones, SIM cards, and other telecommunications equipment contributing significantly to the overall import value of ₹122,356.31 crore.A slicer has been added to filter the data by specific commodities, allowing users to explore detailed import figures for individual electronic goods.

Availability of WIFI in Public & Household Internet Access(%)

The visualization displays data on the availability of public WiFi (measured in count) and the percentage of households with internet access from 2019 to 2024 across various Indian cities, such as Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai, and others. It highlights the increase in both public WiFi infrastructure and household internet access over the years, reflecting the growing digital connectivity in urban areas.

Telecom Subscription Wireless & Wireline (2012-2021)

The visualization illustrates telecom subscription trends in India from 2012 to 2021, highlighting both wireless and wireline subscriptions across various regions, It shows significant growth in wireless subscriptions, with states like Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra leading in subscriber numbers, while wireline subscriptions are comparatively lower but still trackable.A slicer has been added to filter the data by telecom circle and year, enabling users to analyze specific regions or time periods in more detail.
