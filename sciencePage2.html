Science Dashboards

Dive into a vast collection of interactive data visualizations that highlight groundbreaking scientific discoveries, research trends, and critical insights across various disciplines. Our platform brings scientific data to life, making complex phenomena in fields such as physics, biology, chemistry, and astronomy easier to understand through visually captivating displays. From the intricacies of molecular structures to the vastness of space exploration, we present key scientific data in a way that is both engaging and accessible, helping you unravel the mysteries of the natural world and explore the frontiers of human knowledge.

Planetary Information of Milky Way Galaxy

The visualization compares various planetary characteristics, such as diameter, length of day, mean temperature, orbital period, and number of moons across different planets like Jupiter, Saturn, Earth, and Pluto. It provides a detailed comparison of key attributes of the planets in our solar system, showing significant differences in size, rotation, and climate.A slicer allows users to filter the data by planet, enabling focused exploration of individual planetary information.

Space Exploration Missions (1950–2020)

The "Space Exploration Missions (1950–2020)" dashboard offers an in-depth exploration of major space missions over 70 years. It tracks the progression of space exploration, highlighting key missions from different countries .Users can interact with data filters to view missions by year, country, or mission type, enabling a focused analysis of trends in space exploration. Visualizations provide insights into the growth of space programs, major milestones, and the impact of exploration on scientific discovery and innovation.
